## Reading strategy

1. Open the **Long Technical Handbook** (`Overview/handbook_main.pdf`) for
   the week and read its overview chapter. This gives you the *big picture*
   of every term in the week.
2. Open the slide deck. Read it linearly. You will recognise the terms.
3. Try the notebooks in `week_N/notebooks/`.
4. Dive into the external links from the per-week `Readme.md` only when
   you want depth on a specific topic.
