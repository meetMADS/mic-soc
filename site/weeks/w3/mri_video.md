**MRI listens to spins.** In a field `B₀`, net magnetisation precesses at the
**Larmor** frequency `ω₀ = γB₀`; a resonant 90° RF pulse tips it. The **Bloch
equations** give **T1** (recovery) and **T2** (dephasing) — the knobs behind MRI's
soft-tissue contrast. Gradient encoding means the receiver samples **k-space** (the
Fourier transform of the slice); an **inverse FFT** gives the image.
